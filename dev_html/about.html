Nowadays, plants have become an essential part of our lives, yet we rarely pay much attention to their impact on us. Nevertheless, entering a room full of your favourite flowers will brighten up your day instantly! Our team strives to make that possible for everyone. Say, you've taken good care of a big pot of herbs for years, but now you move house and can't take it with you. This website will help you find someone who would love to adopt your plants. Or let's say you've made a few attempts to grow a magnificent orchid but every time you buy one it just dies after a couple of months despite your effort. Now you have the chance to find an orchid looking for a new home and discuss every little detail about its needs with the experienced owner. Whatever your favourite plant is, here you will find one that's just right for you! :)
